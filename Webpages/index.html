About WIE

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network that encourages and supports women engineers and scientists. Our goal is to inspire women to pursue careers in engineering, science, and technology.

Our Mission

  • To empower women in engineering and technology.
  • To organize workshops, events, and technical sessions.
  • To inspire young women to explore STEM fields.
